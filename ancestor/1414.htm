George Winn

  • Born: 1736, VA
  • Died: 1801, Clark Co. KY, at age 65

  General Notes:

George and Lettice Lee Winn purchased land in Fairfax Co. VA before 1765. They removed to Fayette Co. KY in 1784 and received grant of 1,000 acres of land which was surveyed 21 March 1784 where he lived until his death. He sold some land 12 March 1798 to his son, George Jr. and wife Mildred Bullock Winn. He became a member of Coopers Run Baptist Church in Bourbon Co. in 1792. His will dated 20 Feb. 1801 was probabted in Aug. 1803.

To Loving wife Lettice, her choice of one-half of the land bequeathed
to son William to be at her disposal, and the residue of the estate

Executors, wife Lettice WINN, son-in-law John HANCOK, and friend
Edmund BULLOCK. February 20,1801
