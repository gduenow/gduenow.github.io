  General Notes:

[Jonathan.ftw]

She is named as a daughter of Jonathan Park married to a Mr. Winn in the Park Book outline. I have tried to verify her family with other records.

The tombstone for Mary (Park) Winn gives her maiden name as Park. Another researcher sent information that her full name was Mary Eliza/Mary Elizabeth Park. I have found no documentation for any name but Mary.
