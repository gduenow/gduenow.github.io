Survey of Thomas Winn's 400 Acres - 22 MAR 1784. Source: Fayette Co. VA (now KY) Survey Book No. A p. 401
"Fayette County Set: Surveyed for Thomas Winn 400 acres of land by virtue of a Treasury Warrant No. 383 lying on the waters of Stoners Fork of Licking about four miles from Strouds Station & bounded as follows to wit. Beginning at two Red Oaks & White Oak marked HESWP on the west side of the Creek & turning thence S 20 degrees E 400 poles to Elm Sugartree & White Oak on the side of a sink hole hence S 70 degrees E 160 poles crossing a branch to two White Oaks & Elm on the top of a ridge thence at 20 degrees W 400 poles to a Walnut & Locust thence N 70 degrees W 160 poles to the Beginning. March 22nd 1784
John Taylor John South DSFC
Griffin Taylor Tho Marshall SFC
