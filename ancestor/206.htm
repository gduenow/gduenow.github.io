George Johnston 25

  • Born: Abt 1750, Culpeper Co. VA
  • Marriage: Sarah Ross 5 Jun 1770
  • Died: Feb 1836, Randolph Co. MO, about age 86

  General Notes:

He was called in the Revolutionary War in the class of 1781. Removed to Clark Co. KY before 1796 where he purchased and patented land. He removed about 1817 to Howard Co. MO and later to Randolph Co. MO where he lived until he died.
