Loudoun County, Virginia, Deed Abstracts, 1766-1770. Ruth and Sam Sparacio
(Loudoun Co., Va., DB G:59-61) March 31, 1768. John Ashton of Washington Parish, Westmoreland County, to Elizabeth Winn of Cameron Parish, Loudoun County, planter. . . in consideration of rents and covenants .. hath farm let parcel of land in tenure and occupation of said Elizabeth Winn now being, containing 200 acres more or less, being the plantation whereon said Elizabeth Winn hath for some years past dwelt; part of a larger tract of land now in possession of said Elizabeth Winn being in Cameron Parish, Loudoun County on branches of Difficult Run originally taken up and patented by Colo. Henry Ashton .. to have and to hold during natural lives of Elizabeth Winn, Owen Winn, Mary Winn and longest liver of them .. paying every year on first day August the rent of £5 current money of Virginia ..
