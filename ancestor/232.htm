  General Notes:

The sale of property below shows that Thomas Winn was the son of Thomas Winn of Clark Co. KY.

Clark County, Kentucky Deed Book, No. 22, page 363, July 24, 1827.
"Thomas Winn and wife Sarah Winn of Howard County, state of Missouri, sold
to Cornelious Skinner, of Clark County, Ky, 12 and one half Acres of land in
Clark County, Ky, being a portion of land of his father's Thomas Winn, Sr.
dec'd.
Signed -- his
Thomas x Winn
mark

her
Sarah x Winn
mark"


Thomas married Sarah Johnston, daughter of George Johnston and Sarah Ross, on 14 Aug 1796 in Clark Co., Kentucky.14 (Sarah Johnston was born on 20 Jun 1771 in (Orange Co. VA?) and died on 27 Dec 1844 in Macon Co. MO.)
