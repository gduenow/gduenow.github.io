William Johnston

  • Born: 1714, St Peters Parish, New Kent Co. VA
  • Marriage: Jane
  • Died: Between 12 Jun 18 and Aug 1768, Culpeper Co. VA, about age -1696

  General Notes:

He was cared for by the family of Rev. McDaniel after his mother died in 1715. He made oath on February 17, 1735 that he imported himself to Virginia, this in order to secure a grant of land.

• Alt. Birth: Alt. Birth, 1714.

• Alt. Death: Alt. Death. Culpeper Co. VA

• Will, 12 Jun 1768. Signed

• Probate, 18 Aug 1768.
