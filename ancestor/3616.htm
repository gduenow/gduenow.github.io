  General Notes:

Found Albert Jesse Winn in the 1930 U.S. Federal Census, listed as "Lawrence" Winn living at 502 South 5th Street, Columbia, MO with his wife at the time, Florence. Everything else is exactly correct. One can assume that Florence may have given the census taker the information because it lists Albert's parents place of birth as "the United States" instead of Missouri. Perhaps, when the census taker asked his name, she thought he asked hers -- Florence, thus sounding like Lawrence -- My guess. jz
