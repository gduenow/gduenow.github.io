Tabitha Mills 2

  • Born: 27 Feb 1751
  • Marriage: Sr. Ebeneezer Parks 6 Jan 1772
  • Died: 15 Oct 1824, at age 73

  General Notes:

[Jonathan.ftw]

Book by Nell Gum has 18 Oct 1826. Since Ebenezer Park was enumerated on the 1820 census in Madison Co., Kentucky and is believed to be in the household of his son Eli Park in 1830, it is assumed she died in Madison Co., Kentucky.

  Noted events in her life were:

• Alt. Birth: Alt. Birth, 27 Feb 1751. 2,47,48 North Carolina

• Alt. Death: Alt. Death, 15 Oct 1826. 2,47,48 Madison Co., Kentucky


Tabitha married Sr. Ebeneezer Parks, son of John Parke and Mary Davis, on 6 Jan 1772. (Sr. Ebeneezer Parks was born on 12 May 1747 in Virginia 2,47,48,49 and died on 25 Apr 1839 in Estill Co., Kentucky 2,47,48.)

  Noted events in their marriage were:

• Alt. Marriage: Alt. Marriage, 6 Jan 1772. Salisbury, Rowan, North Carolina


  Marriage Notes:

[Jonathan.ftw]

Matt. Troy, John Hankins, bondsmen. The book by Nelly Gum states the marriage bond was signed by John Hawkins and Ebenezer spelled his name as Parke.
