  General Notes:

[Jonathan.ftw]

Jonathan Park, son of Eli Park and Winnafred Dillingham and grandson of Ebenezer Park and Tabitha Mills, recorded in his diary November 14, 1898 that Ebenezer Park son of Ebenezer Park and Tabitha Mills had eight children. He named them as Levi Park married Mary Ann Murphy, John M Park married Patsy Oldham, Turner Park married Winnafred Park a distant relative, Rebecca Park married James Hill, Jemima Park married Ambrose Wagers, Polly Park married Andrew Campbell, Milly Park married Jonathan Scrivner, Emeline Park married Solomon Park a distant cousin.
