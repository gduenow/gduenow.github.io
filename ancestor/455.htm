  General Notes:

[Jonathan.ftw]

He is named as a son of Jonathan Park in the Park Book outline. I have tried to verify his family with other records.

Allen Park married Emily Frances Bratton daughter of Jane Bratton. Jane Bratton is named as an heir of James Coulter in a deed located in Madison Co., Kentucky Book H page 76-79. It is assumed she was a daughter of James Coulter but no marriage was found in Madison Co., Kentucky.

1830 Boone Co., Missouri Census Missouri Township page 115 line 10, Allen Park was living next door (enumerated on line 9) to Richard Pace who is believed to be a son of Jonathan Pace.

Boone County, Missouri Probate Packet 221 Granted 5 August 1835 Final Settlements 15 August 1838 Jane Bratton - Allen Park Administrator: Heirs Emily Park wife of Allen Park, James, Mary and Susan Bratton living in Boone Co., Missouri. Serena Hale wife of Fountain Hale living in Virginia

1840 Boone Co., Missouri Census page 90 Missouri Township Emily Park

Columbia Missouri Statesman dated 16 June 1843 page 2 colume 6

Boone Co., Missouri Probate Packet 477 Guardianship papers on Jesse, Allen, and Nancy Park. Isiah Park guardian. Granted 3 January 1843 with Jonathan Pace as security.

Boone Co., Missouri Probate Packet 751 Guardianship papers on Jesse Park and Allen Park minors with Stephen Bedford Curator August 4, 1847. Allen Park, Jesse Park by Turner McBaine his guardian, and Nancy Runyon

1850 Boone Co., Missouri Census (transcribed by Elizabeth Prather Ellsberry) page 63, FN 948, Presley Stagner age 40 M KY, Emily age 43 F KY, William age 17 M KY, Squire age 15 M KY, Allen Park age 13 M MO, Jesse age 12 M MO, Louisa J. Stagner age 7 F MO.

When his father died he was named between Isaiah and Mary. From census and cemetery records, there dates of birth can be determined. Allen was under 14 in 1822 as his mother was appointed guardian at that time.
