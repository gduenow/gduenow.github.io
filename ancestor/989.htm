  General Notes:

[Jonathan.ftw]

Some researchers have assigned her with the maiden name of Davis. This comes from one reference in the estate settlement for her first husband John Park. In 1771 she was listed as Mary Davis, widow of John Park. Davis would be her married name not her maiden name.

"N.d. Reserve 1/3 to Mary Davis, Widdow of Jno Park. 1771."


Mary married John Parke, son of John Parke and Sarah Smith, about 1729.2 (John Parke was born about 1707 in Hopewell Twp, Hunterdon, Colony Of West Jersey 2,125 and died in Feb 1758 near Cumberland, Maryland 2,125.)
