Hergelzhumar has no moon.

Each year is composed of 251 days.

Each day is 32 hours long.

The year is broken into Spring/Summer/Fall/Winter.
Spring and Fall are 60 days each.
Summer and Winter are 65 days long.
1 spare day is called Seer's day and is between Winter and Spring.

Weeks are 5 days long:
Vulpesday	Fox
Alcesday	Moose
Ursusday	Bear
Guloday		Wolverine
Sorexday	Shrew	(Rest)
