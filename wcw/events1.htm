CODCON

Three things are certain: Death, Taxes and Diplomacy.

Just another reminder that our open Diplomacy tournament at CODCon is fast approaching. Here's what you need to know:

When: Learning round Friday, April 13; tournament Saturday, April 14, and Sunday, April 15.

Where: CODCon will be held at the College of DuPage in Glen Ellyn. You can find the address, housing options, etc., online at http://codcon.com. Please note that CODCon is an annual gaming convention sponsored by the College of DuPage Sci-Fi/Fantasy Club. The con expects about 400 attendees.

Cost: The convention fee at the door is $10 for all three days or $5 for one day. If you preregister at http://codcon.com by March 31, you can go all three days for only $8 or spend $4 for one day. In addition to the convention fee, we will be charging a $10 tournament entry fee, to be payable to Chief Financial Weasel Thom Comstock or myself at the tournament site. We will waive our tournament fee for kids and students. (We're not charging a fee for the learning round.) This fee will help offset the cost of the awards. See below.

What: A three-round Diplomacy tournament, with the best two rounds counting for score. We'd love to have you participate in all three rounds, but even if you only come out for one, it would be a great help to our club. Scoring system will be center-based, emphasizing center count over draw size. All rounds will be timed and completed in 5 hours and 45 minutes or less. Here's the schedule:
