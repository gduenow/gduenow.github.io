Calling All Weasels!

The Windy City Weasels’ Tournament Committee cordially invites you to attend Weasel Moot II, a three-round Diplomacy tournament to be held November 14th, 15th and 16th in Chicago.

What:A three-round Diplomacy tournament, and the eighteenth stop on the North American Diplomacy Federation’s 2008 Grand Prix Circuit.

When:November 14th, 15th and 16th, 2008

Where:At the Days Inn Lincoln Park, 644 West Diversey Parkway,Chicago, IL 60614.

Hotel Info

Who:The Windy City Weasels is a Chicago area club dedicated to the play of the classic boardgame Diplomacy. We’ve been organizing games since September 2005. This is our club’s second annual tournament.

Jeremiah Peterson serves as Tournament Director. Jim O'Kelley, Kevin O'Kelly, Thom Comstock and Greg Duenow round out the tournament committee.

How Much The preregistration fee is $35, payable by PayPal to Thom Comstock at Redpawn3@yahoo.com. Just provide your name and mention Weasel Moot when you send the funds. We’ll charge $40 at the door.

Schedule
  • Round One:
    Friday, November 14
    Registration: 6 to 6:30 p.m.
    Round Begins: 6:45 p.m.

  • Round Two:
    Saturday, November 15
    Registration: 9:15 to 9:30 a.m.
    Round Begins: 9:45 a.m.

  • Round Three:
    Saturday, November 15
    Registration: 5:30 to 5:45 p.m.
    Round Begins: 6 p.m.

  • Round 3 will end at a time known only to the tournament director between 3 a.m. and 5 a.m.

  • Awards Ceremony
    The awards ceremony will be Sunday, November 16 at 9 a.m.
