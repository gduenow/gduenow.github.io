From O'Hare Airport

Take the Blue Line south to the Jackson stop

Transfer to the Red Line

Take the Howard (north bound) train to the Fullerton stop

Transfer to the Brown line

Get on the Kimball (north bound) train

Go one stop and get off at Diversey

Walk a block and a half east to the Days Inn
