The Woodrows:

Americana/Rock/Folk/Country

Chicago, IL

Steve Baker: Vocals, guitar, slide.

Steve has been a consistent contributor to the Old Town School of Folk Music’s Gram Parson tribute show.

Founding member of Chicago band Cowbell.

Influences: Steve Earle, Jayhawks

Ric Salazar: Vocals, guitar, b-bender.

Ric has been playing in Chicago for many years.  Projects include: the Stillweeds; R. D. Roth; and Pamela Richardson and the Gentleman Callers.

Performed at Nevin’s Gene Clark Tribute show with The Vagabonds.

Influences: Gene Clark, Calexico

Andy Abrisz:  Drums, vocals.

Andy was in Iowa bands the Mayflies (now the Letterpress Opry) and the Dick Prall Band.

Influences: Wilco, the Muppets 		 

Greg Duenow:  Bass.

Greg was member of Missouri bands the Wheelie Buddies (now El Mail Boxo) and Crack School.

Influences: Willie Nelson, Flat Duo Jets
